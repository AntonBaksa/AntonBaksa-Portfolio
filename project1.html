Overview

Dream Dash is a fast-paced competitive infinite runner where two players race through dream-inspired environments, collecting shards and avoiding obstacles to outscore their opponent. Dream Dash appeals to players who typically enjoy a quick ''jump in to action'' style of game where they can play it multiple times to beat their previous high scores.

  • Collect as many dream shards as you can within 2 minutes
  • Avoid obstacles as you lose points when running in to them
  • Push your opponent out of thw way to gain advatage
  • Collect power ups to help out. There are 3 different power ups to collect
  • At the end you get to see statistics for both players of how they played
